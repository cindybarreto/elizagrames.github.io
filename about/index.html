About Me

Hi, I'm Eliza Grames. I'm pursuing a PhD in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at the Univeristy of Connecticut under the advisement of Dr. Chris Elphick. I'm studying mechanisms underlying area sensitivity in birds breeding in southern New England forest fragments. I am particularly interested in the impacts of urbanization on bird populations.

You can read more about my prior work experience and research on other sections of my web page.
